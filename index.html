Typing Kinematics

Cognitive load affects motor control. When stressed, people tend to type with higher variance in timing (rhythm) and make more uncorrected errors. A smooth, rhythmic typing pattern usually indicates a state of flow or low stress.

Usage Context

Excessive screen time, late-night device usage (disrupting circadian rhythms), and frequent context-switching (app hopping) are strong behavioural correlates with heightened anxiety and stress levels.
